## What it was

DailyRounds is India's leading medical content platform; Marrow is its NEET-PG preparation product. I joined early and spent two and a half years as the medical lead: primary clinical voice on the product team, operations lead for twenty medical editors across four specialty teams.

## What I did

I designed and launched the flagship "Pearls" feature — indexed, searchable clinical content for rapid exam revision. I was the primary clinical liaison between the engineering team and physician users, translating workflow into product requirements. I drove content standards, QA processes, and the operational rhythms that let a small editorial team produce enough high-quality content to serve half a million users.

## What I learned

How to scale a clinical content operation. How to sit between engineers and doctors and make both sides productive. How product decisions that seem small — indexing, cross-linking, micro-chunk revision — compound at scale.
