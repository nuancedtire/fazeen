## What it is

A dedicated microsite and custom browser-based data-collection tool for the quality improvement project I co-lead at Whipps Cross A&E on mental health pathway compliance. The tool replaces paper-form audits that nobody completes with a frictionless digital capture flow — built to be filled in on a ward computer in under a minute.

## Why I built it

Manual audit data collection was a key limiter of compliance visibility. The usual advice — "do a better audit" — doesn't work when the bottleneck is data-entry friction. So I built the thing we actually needed.

## Where it's at

In active use at Whipps Cross A&E, data collection phase, with departmental presentation and re-audit cycle planned on completion.
