## What they are

Peerr.io is a secure, regulated professional network for health and life sciences — think of it as a purpose-built social layer for clinicians, healthcare, and medical affairs professionals, with content tooling, career features, and AI throughout. Aiigent.io is the sister platform on the same technical foundation, hosting more experimental clinical workflow tools like **Compass** (compass.aiigent.io) and **NEA** (nea.aiigent.io).

## My role

Founding engineer across both platforms where I continue to serve on a consultancy basis. I've shipped the LLM-powered features — news summarisation, job screening, content curation — using structured outputs and prompt engineering tuned for clinical accuracy. I'm responsible for SOC 2 and GDPR compliance posture across the platform: data handling, consent, and content moderation appropriate to a clinical user base.

## Where it's at

Peerr: 15,000+ registered users, ~3,000 monthly active. Aiigent: in active development with Compass and NEA both live. Both platforms continue to ship new features frequently.
