## What it was

A PyTorch-based CNN trained to prioritise chest X-rays by urgency — one of my earliest forays into medical deep learning, built during internship. Crude by today's standards; formative for me.

## What it became

The start of a seven-year thread through my work. Every clinical AI project I've built since — including NeurallyMed, AskNICE, and Aide — has drawn on lessons from that first model.
