## What it is

A retrieval-augmented generation interface over NICE clinical guidelines. Ask a question in plain English, get a source-cited answer drawing from the NICE corpus. Built in 2023 as a personal tool; kept online as a reference implementation of clinical RAG.

## Why I built it

Searching NICE guidelines is painful — the site's own search engine works poorly for the way clinicians actually ask questions. A small LLM wrapper with decent retrieval solves most of the problem.
