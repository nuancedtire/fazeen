## What it was

An AI-powered exam preparation platform for the PLAB (Professional and Linguistic Assessments Board), the pathway international medical graduates take to practise in the UK. Adaptive question generation, AI-assisted revision, personalised weakness targeting.

## What happened

Built independently; acquired in 2023.

## What I learned

Building an education product for doctors is as much about trust and content provenance as it is about the AI. The features that retained users were always the ones that felt obviously correct, not the ones that felt technically impressive.
