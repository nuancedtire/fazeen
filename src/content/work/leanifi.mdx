## What it is

Leanifi is a personal AI-powered fitness and nutrition assistant. Unlike conventional calorie trackers, it acts as a conversational coach — you tell it what you ate, how you trained, how you're feeling, and it responds with context-aware guidance, adjustments, and accountability. The focus is on building sustainable habits rather than precise macro accounting.

## Why I built it

I wanted a tool that felt like talking to a knowledgeable friend rather than filling in a spreadsheet. The existing apps are either too rigid (precise logging for people who enjoy logging) or too generic (chatbots with no memory of your history). Leanifi sits in the gap — opinionated enough to be useful, conversational enough to stick with.
