## What it was

A deep-learning model trained to verify correct endotracheal tube placement on neonatal chest X-rays — one of the most common and highest-stakes procedures in neonatology. Built as a proof-of-concept while I was in the early stages of my career.

## What I learned

The gap between a model that works on a curated dataset and a model that would be safe in a NICU is enormous. This is the project that first taught me that the interesting problems in clinical AI are implementation problems.
