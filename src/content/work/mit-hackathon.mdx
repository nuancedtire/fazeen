## What it was

A 48-hour hackathon hosted by MIT in the early months of the pandemic, bringing clinicians and engineers together to build tools that could help during the crisis. Our team placed second in the clinical AI triage track.

## What I contributed

Clinical framing and AI model work on a chest imaging triage prototype, building on the CNN work I'd started in 2018.
